Hi, I’m Andy, a designer with over 12 years experience working with clients ranging from Nike to Nestle. Originally from the North East of England, I now live in Hackney, London.

I’ve been interested in design and technology for as long as I can remember and I love that I get to work in the intersection of the two.

I’m an experienced leader of designers and developers, solving complex problems with simple solutions.

As global head of product at Havas, I’ve collaborated with people in over 25 countries (visiting many) creating websites, native mobile apps and ad creatives.

My experience in ad production (900+ campaigns at Havas) led me to co-found Basis Ads with Michael Loistl to help marketers and designers produce video and interactive creatives.

Away from the screen I enjoy going for walks with my dog, cooking along with YouTube chefs, travelling with my girlfriend and trying to play football with my pub team.

I’m currently looking for full-time opportunities based in London or remotely. Let’s find a time to chat:
